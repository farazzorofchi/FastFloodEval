Recently, federal emergency management agency (FEMA) published its flood claim experience data. The dataset represents more than two million claim transactions from 1970 to 2019. Machine learning and deep learning techniques were applied to this dataset in order to provide a sound approach to quantify the loss ratio (loss / insured value) of buildings with different attributes using historical claim data.

By utilizing this web-app, flood insurance companies and people who are interested in buying a property can better evaluate the risk of flooding for the structure. Insurance companies can leverage this tool to evaluate and decide if they want to:

1- Accept the risk.
2- Mitigate the risk by increasing the premiums.
3- Avoid writing the risk.

Homeowners/homebuyers can also leverage this tool to decide whether they need to purchase flood insurance.

There are many other attributes that can cause flooding. This tool only uses the historical claims data and doesn't guarantee the results to be accurate.
